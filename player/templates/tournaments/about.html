About

Touch DB (currently beta v0.1) is an attempt to put touch tournaments information online.

Features

Touch DB currenlty features are tournament data in html format which can be navigate, clicking in the different links to see games, teams and player information.

Planned features

Players and team search is soon available.

Available tournaments

Berlin Capital Cup 2014 and NTS 2014 are already available. Upcoming Capital Cup 2015.

How to colaborate

We are a social app and want to be social, you can collaborate with feeding us with new input data or any new ideas. Some examples of how you can collaborate with us:

  • Would you like to have your tournament online?
  • Would you like to report wrong information?
  • Did you find a Bug and you would like to report it?

Go to the contact site and get in touch with us.

Who we are?

We are a bunch of touch Berlin players with IT background and a passion for playing touch :)
