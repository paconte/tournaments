About

Touch DB purpose is to put touch tournaments data online. We also offer tournaments organizers a tool to display tournaments data online in real time.

Features

  • Surf online touch tournaments data: fixtures, statistics, Players.
  • Search your for your favorite team or players to find out their tournament performance.
  • Add your own tournament data to the app and it will be immediately online ready.

How to colaborate

We are a social app and want to be social, you can collaborate with feeding us with new input data or any new ideas. Some examples of how you can collaborate with us:

  • Would you like to have your tournament online?
  • Would you like to report wrong information?
  • Did you find a Bug and you would like to report it?

Go to the contact site and get in touch with us.

Planned features

  • Tournament data for Berlin Capital Cup 2015 will be soon available.
  • Player statistic will be soon available.
  • If you want to request your own feature go to contact site and get in touch with us.
